About

Ashong-Nartey is a researcher at the school of Information and Software Engineering, UESTC. He has a successful track record in conducting high-profiled researches that have been published in high-ranking journals and conferences. Prior to his current position, He was a doctoral student at the School of Computer Science and Engineering, where he researched on deep semi-supervised learning algorithms for small dataset problems. He published his findings in high-ranking journals. He also worked as Teaching assistant and project coordinator at the Departments of Computer Science and Computer Engineering, All Nations University, Ghana. He has received Computer Science and Technology degrees from University of Electronic Science and Technology of China (Ph.D., M.Sc.) and Computer Science Honours degree from All Nations University (B.Sc. Hons.).
